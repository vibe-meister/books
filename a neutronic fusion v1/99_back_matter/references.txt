REFERENCES AND FURTHER READING

General Fusion and Plasma Physics

1. Chen, Francis F. "Introduction to Plasma Physics and Controlled Fusion." 3rd ed. Springer, 2016.
   - Comprehensive textbook on plasma physics and fusion

2. Freidberg, Jeffrey P. "Plasma Physics and Fusion Energy." Cambridge University Press, 2007.
   - Detailed treatment of fusion physics and engineering

3. Wesson, John. "Tokamaks." 4th ed. Oxford University Press, 2011.
   - Authoritative reference on tokamak physics

Aneutronic Fusion

4. Hora, Heinrich, et al. "Road map to clean energy using laser beam ignition of boron-hydrogen fusion." Laser and Particle Beams, vol. 35, no. 4, 2017, pp. 730-740.
   - Overview of laser-driven p-11B fusion

5. Miley, George H., and Heinrich Hora. "Aneutronic fusion rocket based on proton-boron-11." Journal of Physics: Conference Series, vol. 717, 2016.
   - Application of p-11B fusion to space propulsion

6. Rostoker, Norman, Michl Binderbauer, and Hendrik J. Monkhorst. "Colliding beam fusion reactor." Science, vol. 278, no. 5342, 1997, pp. 1419-1422.
   - Early work on beam-target aneutronic fusion

Proton-Boron-11 Fusion

7. Labaune, C., et al. "Fusion reactions initiated by laser-accelerated particle beams in a laser-produced plasma." Nature Communications, vol. 4, 2013, article 2506.
   - Experimental demonstration of laser-driven p-11B fusion

8. Belloni, F., et al. "Proton-boron fusion rate in high-energy-density plasmas." Physical Review E, vol. 94, 2016, article 013206.
   - Cross-section measurements and calculations

9. Eliezer, Shalom, et al. "Boron-proton nuclear-fusion laser plasma interactions." Plasma Physics and Controlled Fusion, vol. 58, no. 3, 2016, article 034009.
   - Review of p-11B fusion research

Deuterium-Helium-3 Fusion

10. Santarius, John F., and Gerald L. Kulcinski. "Helium-3 resources and acquisition for terrestrial fusion energy." Fusion Technology, vol. 28, no. 4, 1995, pp. 1725-1731.
    - Discussion of helium-3 resources and applications

11. Wurden, G. A., et al. "Helium-3 fusion: A path to a clean energy future." Journal of Fusion Energy, vol. 35, 2016, pp. 85-92.
    - Overview of D-³He fusion prospects

Bremsstrahlung and Energy Loss

12. Huba, J. D. "NRL Plasma Formulary." Naval Research Laboratory, 2013.
    - Comprehensive reference for plasma physics formulas

13. Putvinski, S., et al. "Physics of advanced tokamak regimes for burning plasma experiments." Nuclear Fusion, vol. 47, 2007, pp. S427-S432.
    - Discussion of bremsstrahlung and other energy loss mechanisms

Direct Energy Conversion

14. Momota, Hiromu, et al. "Conceptual design of the D-³He reactor ARTEMIS." Fusion Technology, vol. 21, no. 4, 1992, pp. 2307-2323.
    - Design study including direct energy conversion

15. Miley, George H., et al. "Advanced fusion energy conversion." Fusion Technology, vol. 39, no. 2, 2001, pp. 480-485.
    - Review of direct energy conversion methods

Space Propulsion

16. Slough, John, et al. "Fusion propulsion for interplanetary travel." Journal of Propulsion and Power, vol. 30, no. 4, 2014, pp. 1017-1025.
    - Application of fusion to space propulsion

17. Cassibry, Jason T., et al. "Scaling laws for pulsed fusion propulsion." Journal of Propulsion and Power, vol. 32, no. 5, 2016, pp. 1204-1213.
    - Analysis of fusion rocket scaling

Current Research Projects

18. TAE Technologies. "The Path to Commercial Fusion Energy." Various publications and presentations.
    - Information on field-reversed configuration research

19. HB11 Energy. "Laser-Driven Fusion Energy." Company publications and research papers.
    - Information on laser-driven p-11B fusion

20. LPP Fusion. "Dense Plasma Focus Research." Various publications.
    - Information on plasma focus aneutronic fusion research
