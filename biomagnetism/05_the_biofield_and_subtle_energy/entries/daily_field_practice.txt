Historical roots:
Daily practice routines appear in ancient traditions as complete systems for energy cultivation, including morning and evening practices in yoga, qigong, and various spiritual traditions. These routines integrated multiple techniques for working with "energy" or "life force." Modern research validates that regular practice produces measurable improvements in HRV, brain activity, and health, corresponding to optimized field patterns.
